What is MERIDIAN?

MERIDIAN is a living laboratory leadership resource built from the real experiences of SRHS laboratory managers and supervisors. It exists because the best guidance for a leader in a difficult moment comes not from a policy manual, but from a colleague who has been there before.

The name reflects the tool's purpose — a meridian is a fixed reference line that orients everything else on a map. When you're navigating something unfamiliar, MERIDIAN is your reference point.

Two tools in one
Leadership situations
Real scenarios from real managers — what happened, what was tried, what worked, what didn't, and what to do next time.
Process guides
Step-by-step instructions for every operational task — Strata reports, Phenom requisitions, UKG transfers, invoice processing, and more.
Part of the Lab Leadership Continuum

MERIDIAN is the interactive resource component of the Lab Leadership Continuum — a system-wide supervisor and manager development framework built for Spartanburg Regional Health System's four outlying laboratory campuses: Cherokee Medical Center, Mary Black Campus, Pelham Medical Center, and Union Medical Center.
